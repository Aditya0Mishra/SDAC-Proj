About Us

Industry-First Innovations
Understanding and acting upon the consumer pulse have always driven MuscleBlaze® to provide industry-first solutions. MuscleBlaze® was the first in India to introduce an SMS based product authenticity mechanism supported by a protein test certificate from NABL certified labs. MuscleBlaze® was also the first to introduce a patented Scoop-on-Top design to end the scoop tussle.
